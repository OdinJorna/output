Waar voldoet een goede sessie aan?

 31st March 2023 at 8:30pm 
Geheim
Combat:Challenge the players' battlefield strategy with a unique, fun, and meaningful combat encounter.
Options:Provide the players with multiple different choices and methods to go about achieving a goal.
Role-play:Give players the chance to socialize. Connect with their backstory or further their characters' goals.
Puzzle:Have the players solve a riddle, brain-teaser, environmental puzzle, or story-based mystery.
Story:Hook the players with an objective, establish an opponent, create conflict, and build to a climax.
Exploration:Allow the players to discover new locations, NPCs, monsters, environments, and hazards.
Spoils:Reward the players with gold, magic items, information, or some other prize for their efforts.
