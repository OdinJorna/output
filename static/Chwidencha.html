Chwidencha

 28th July 2023 at 9:20am 

Web-Dweller. The chwidencha can climb difficult surfaces, including upside down on ceilings and across webs, without penalty. While in contact with a web, the chwidencha knows the exact location of any other creature in contact with the same web.

Burrowing Ambusher. The chwidencha has advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks made to hide while at least partially buried in loam or dark soil.

Fruitless Devotion. If a creature the chwidencha can see uses its action to brandish a holy symbol of Lolth, the chwidencha must succeed on a DC 15 Wisdom saving throw or be Charmed and Frightened by it until the beginning of the creature's next turn.

Actions

Multiattack. The spider makes six attacks, or makes two attacks and uses its Nettling Bristles, if available.

Leg Barbs. Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. Hit: 6 (1d6 + 3) bludgeoning damage. Instead of dealing damage, the Chwidencha may grapple the target.

Scuttling Overrun. The chwidencha moves up to half its speed, and may move through the space of any Large or smaller creature. A creature whose space it enters for the first time on a turn must succeeed on a DC 15 Wisdom saving throw or fall prone and be Frightened for the next minute. While Frightened in this way, a creature can stand from prone only as an action, and repeats this saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on a success.

Nettling Bristles (Recharge 5-6). The chwidencha scrapes off some of its barbed bristles, ejecting them in a 15 foot cone. Each creature in the area must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or take 14 (4d6) piercing damage and be Blinded until it takes an action to clear its eyes, or take half as much damage on a success.

Reactions

Buried Horror. As a reaction when a creature the chwidencha is hidden from enters its reach, the chwidencha uses its Scuttling Overrun or makes four attacks with its Leg Barbs.
